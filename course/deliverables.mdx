## How to Use These Resources

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with the Progress Tracker">
    Download the Progress Tracker to mark your journey through all 12 modules.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download as You Go">
    Each module's "Deliverables" section links to relevant tools. Download them when you reach that module.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Print or Digital">
    All resources work in both formats:
    - **Print:** Perfect for daily tracking and journaling
    - **Digital:** Use in Excel, Google Sheets, or note-taking apps
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize">
    These templates are starting points. Adapt them to fit YOUR needs and preferences.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Resources are released with each module.**

  Some resources are available now (Progress Tracker, tracking sheets), while others unlock as you progress through the course. This ensures you're not overwhelmed and receive tools exactly when you need them.
</Note>
