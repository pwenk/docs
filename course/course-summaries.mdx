## Module 1: Take Stock + Why Everything You Know Is Wrong

**Duration:** 30 minutes | **Priority:** ⭐⭐

### The Bottom Line
Most nutrition advice is fundamentally wrong. Your first step is observing your current state without judgment.

### Action Items
- Complete 3-day Food & Feeling Journal
- Document all nutrition beliefs you currently hold
- Shift mindset from "I know" to "I'm experimenting"

## Module 2: The PUFA Problem (The Real Enemy)

**Duration:** 45 minutes | **Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐ MOST IMPORTANT

### The Bottom Line
Seed oils (PUFAs) are the real problem, not sugar. They destroy thyroid function and metabolism.

### Action Items
- Perform kitchen PUFA audit
- Practice label reading at grocery store
- Learn restaurant PUFA detection strategies

**Duration:** 45 minutes | **Priority:** ⭐⭐⭐
